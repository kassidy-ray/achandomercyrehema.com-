Wet Fry Tilapia with Ugali & Greens

Ingredients:

  • Tilapia fish
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic & Ginger
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt & spices
  • Ugali (maize flour)
  • Kale/Spinach

Instructions:

Fry onions, garlic, and ginger. Add tomatoes and spices, then cook tilapia in the mixture. Prepare ugali and greens separately.

Nutritional Information:

Approximately 600 calories per serving.

Chips with Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Chicken pieces
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt & spices
  • Garlic & herbs

Instructions:

Fry the potatoes until golden. Marinate and grill or fry the chicken with spices and garlic.

Nutritional Information:

Approximately 700 calories per serving.
