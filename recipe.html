Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Egg yolks
  • Pancetta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

Boil the pasta, cook the pancetta, and mix with egg yolks and parmesan. Combine with pasta and season with black pepper.

Nutritional Information:

Approximately 500 calories per serving.

Grilled Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Salmon fillet
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Herbs

Instructions:

Marinate the salmon in olive oil, garlic, and herbs. Grill for 10-15 minutes and finish with lemon juice.

Nutritional Information:

Approximately 300 calories per serving.
